# Family Law Firm Marketing Website

## What This Is

A single-page marketing website for a specialized family law firm. The site showcases the firm's expertise in family law and converts visitors into leads through prominent contact options.

## Core Value

Visitors can quickly understand the firm's family law expertise and easily initiate contact via phone or contact form.

- [ ] Mobile-responsive design

- Primary conversion goal: phone calls and form submissions
